# Product

## Register

product

## Users
- **Maritime Fleet Managers & Operations Teams**: Managing survey scheduling, tracking validity of multiple documents across vessels under time constraints and compliance pressure.
- **Vessel Crew & Captains**: Accessing/uploading documents directly from shipboard environments with potentially limited connectivity and smaller devices (mobile/tablet).
- **Office Teams / Watch Officers**: Monitoring real-time compliance status on wall-mounted TV screens (TV mode dashboard).

## Product Purpose
- Provide a clear, reliable, real-time overview of vessel certificates and survey statuses.
- Prevent costly vessel detentions by alerting users about upcoming expirations, due dates, and overdue actions.
- Facilitate easy document management and audit trails for compliance.

## Brand Personality
- **Reliable**: Secure, robust, and dependable—critical for safety and regulatory compliance.
- **Clear**: Minimal distraction, allowing important details to stand out immediately.
- **Maritime-Utility**: Fusing professional maritime aesthetics (Spruce Green, Gold) with clean status tracking.

## Anti-references
- **Flashing/Alarmist Dashboards**: UIs that cause panic with constant alerts and overly bright colors.
- **Cluttered SaaS Templates**: Standard generic dashboards filled with unnecessary widgets and graphs.
- **Low-Contrast Interfaces**: Gray-on-gray elements that are difficult to read on mobile or under office lighting.

## Design Principles
- **Glanceable Status**: A user looking at a TV dashboard from across the room should instantly identify if any vessel is in an "Expired" or "Due" state.
- **High Information Density with Visual Breathing Room**: Essential certification details must be shown without overwhelming the operator, utilizing balanced spacing.
- **Resilient and Clear Alert hierarchy**: Use clear color-coding (Gold for primary actions/alerts, Green for active/valid, Red for critical/overdue) supported by robust iconography to ensure readability for colorblind users.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG 2.1 AA compliant contrast ratios across dark and light modes.
- Colorblind-friendly design: Statuses must be differentiated by icons (Check, Warning, Alert) and text, not just colors.
- Responsive layout supporting small ship-cabin screens, standard office monitors, and wall-mounted TV displays.
